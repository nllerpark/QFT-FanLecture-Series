## 🎯 Project Description

> *Exploding QFT: A Fan-Driven Lecture Series* is a creative and educational quantum physics project that blends rigorous theoretical exposition with narrative elements from popular culture.
>
> These lectures use fictionalized courtroom drama and anime characters to explore deep ideas in quantum mechanics such as state superposition, measurement theory, energy quantization, decoherence, causality, observer-relative reality, and quantum information.
>
> Current lectures cover quantum superposition and measurement, quantization and decoherence, and observer-dependent reality through the Wigner's Friend framework.
>
> Designed for learners with a background in physics or an interest in fan-based science storytelling, this series bridges formal quantum mechanics and intuitive narrative.
>
> All historical physicists, scientific concepts, and fictional characters referenced in this series are publicly documented and may be consulted through standard textbooks or English Wikipedia.  
> This material is released under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) License.
